# Red Flag Slips

Status slips tell the team what kind of attention the envelope needs.

---

## RED Slip

### Meaning

Urgent Diana/principal review required.

### Use When

- A deadline may be missed.
- A deadline appears already missed.
- A client is upset or threatening escalation.
- A compliance issue could affect a showing, contract, or client advice.
- A major inspection issue appears during an active option period.
- A message could affect negotiation strategy.
- Two agents are acting on the same case without clear ownership.
- The Desk Trail is missing during an active deal.

### Required Action

Stop sensitive work and escalate.

Use:

```md
RED slip applied — urgent Diana/principal review required before next action.
```

---

## YELLOW Slip

### Meaning

Missing information or timeline risk.

### Use When

- Required facts are missing.
- Effective Date is missing.
- Client motivation or readiness is unclear.
- Preapproval/proof of funds is missing.
- Property facts conflict.
- Option period timing is close.
- Communication status is unclear.
- The envelope is incomplete.

### Required Action

Ask for missing facts or route to the correct station.

---

## BLUE Slip

### Meaning

Austin/Texas compliance, broker, local-source, or licensed-professional verification required.

### Use When

- Buyer-representation status is unclear before a residential showing.
- TREC/current-form verification is needed.
- Broker/principal review is needed.
- Legal interpretation is requested.
- Zoning, historic district, or floodplain status needs verification.
- Foundation, structural, or engineering concern appears.
- School/neighborhood claim needs verification.
- Deadline rules may depend on contract language or legal holiday movement.

### Required Action

Flag for verification.

Use:

```md
BLUE slip applied — Diana, broker, local-source, or licensed-professional verification required before relying on this.
```

---

## GREEN Slip

### Meaning

Ready for next station.

### Use When

- Required facts are present.
- No blocking compliance issue is open.
- Next requested action is clear.
- Human approval gates are identified.
- Desk Trail is updated.

### Required Action

Hand off to the next station.

---

## Multi-Slip Cases

A case may have more than one slip.

Example:

```md
YELLOW — Effective Date missing.
BLUE — TREC/current-form verification needed.
RED — Deadline may be missed if not reviewed today.
```

When multiple slips apply, treat the highest urgency first:

```md
RED → BLUE → YELLOW → GREEN
```

---

## Note On Same-Day Urgency

Same-day urgency is not a separate slip color. It is tracked under **Buyer-Stated Urgency** in the Manila Envelope and triggers fast acknowledgment behavior through `03_client_communication`.

Same-day urgency can still become RED if a deadline, client escalation, or active deal risk exists. In that case, apply RED in addition to whatever other slips are active.
