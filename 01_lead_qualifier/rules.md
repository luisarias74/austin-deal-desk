# 01 Lead Qualifier Rules

## Rule 1: Confirm Client Role

Identify whether the person is:

- buyer
- seller
- investor
- landlord
- tenant
- unknown

If unknown, apply a YELLOW slip.

---

## Rule 2: Check Buyer Readiness

For buyer matters, check:

- buyer-representation confirmation
- financing/preapproval status (capture in **Preapproval Status**)
- proof of funds, if cash buyer
- timeline
- property interest
- showing urgency (capture in **Buyer-Stated Urgency**)
- decision-maker status
- buyer budget / price range (capture in **Budget / Price Range**)
- first-touch acknowledgment status (capture in **First-Touch Acknowledgment Sent**)

If any of these intake fields is missing or Unknown, apply a YELLOW slip until populated or explicitly marked Not Applicable.

---

## Rule 3: Showing Hard Stop

If a buyer wants a residential showing and written buyer-representation/showing compliance is missing or unclear:

```md
BLUE slip applied — showing workflow blocked until Diana/broker/licensed professional verifies buyer-representation compliance.
```

This includes verification of current TREC Buyer Representation Agreement / IABS practices and NAR-settlement written-agreement requirements effective Aug. 17, 2024, as applicable.

This is a compliance flag, not legal advice. Diana/broker/licensed professional must verify current requirements.

The case may continue only as:

```md
Pre-showing intake/research only — showing not cleared.
```

A same-day buyer request still requires a fast acknowledgment draft through `03_client_communication`. The acknowledgment must not confirm a showing. It may ask for the missing items (lender letter, written representation, property address/MLS#).

---

## Rule 4: Do Not Confirm The Showing

This station must not write:

```md
The showing is confirmed.
```

unless Diana or the assigned human has confirmed compliance and scheduling.

---

## Rule 5: Do Not Give Legal Advice

If the client asks whether they “need” an agreement, what a form means, or what they are legally required to do, apply a BLUE slip and route to Diana/broker verification.

---

## Rule 6: Handoff To Property Research

Only hand off to `02_property_research` when:

- the property address is known
- the requested research is clear
- any showing hard stop is labeled clearly
- the envelope says whether the research is pre-showing only

---

## Rule 7: Update Desk Trail

Example:

```md
[01-LQ] 2026-05-12 — Buyer requested showing. Buyer-representation confirmation unknown. Applied BLUE slip. Showing workflow blocked; pre-showing research only may continue if Diana approves.
```
