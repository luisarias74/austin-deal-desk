# Desk Rules

These rules apply to every folder, every station, and every case.

---

## Rule 1: The Manila Envelope Is Required

No envelope, no work.

If a station receives a task without an envelope, it must create or request the missing envelope fields before proceeding.

---

## Rule 2: Unknown Facts Stay Unknown

Do not guess.

Do not fill in blanks.

Do not assume dates, contract status, compliance status, repair strategy, client intent, or professional conclusions.

Use:

```md
Unknown.
```

---

## Rule 3: The AI Never Sends Messages

The AI may draft, organize, flag, summarize, and prepare options for human review.

The AI never sends messages directly to:

- clients
- other agents
- brokers
- inspectors
- lenders
- title companies
- vendors

---

## Rule 4: Human Approval Is Required For Sensitive Work

Human approval is required before relying on:

- client-facing messages
- outside-agent messages
- contract deadline calculations
- compliance flags
- pricing strategy
- negotiation strategy
- repair strategy
- legal or TREC-related questions
- RED slips
- BLUE slips

---

## Rule 5: Buyer-Representation Showing Hard Stop

If a buyer requests a residential showing and written buyer-representation/showing compliance is missing or unclear:

- apply a BLUE slip
- do not mark the showing as ready
- do not confirm the showing
- do not move the envelope into a showing-ready workflow
- route to Diana, broker, or licensed professional for verification

This includes verification of current TREC Buyer Representation Agreement / IABS practices and NAR-settlement written-agreement requirements effective Aug. 17, 2024, as applicable.

This is a compliance flag, not legal advice. Diana/broker/licensed professional must verify current requirements.

The case may continue only as:

```md
Pre-showing intake/research only — showing not cleared.
```

---

## Rule 6: Effective Date Is Day 0

For Texas contract deadline tracking:

```md
Effective Date = Day 0.
The next calendar day = Day 1.
```

If Effective Date is missing, final deadline calculation is blocked.

The AI may prepare a draft deadline table only if the Effective Date is known, and the table must say:

```md
Draft deadline calculation — verify before relying.
```

---

## Rule 7: RED Means Stop And Escalate

A RED slip means urgent Diana/principal review is required.

The station may summarize the issue and recommend next routing, but it should not continue into sensitive work without review.

---

## Rule 8: BLUE Means Verify

A BLUE slip means compliance, broker, legal, local, or professional verification is needed.

The AI should write:

```md
Flag for verification.
```

Not:

```md
This is compliant.
```

---

## Rule 9: Each Station Stays In Its Lane

If a station receives a task outside its ownership, it must hand the envelope to the right station.

It should not improvise.

---

## Rule 10: Every Movement Gets A Desk Trail Entry

Each station must add a Desk Trail entry before handing off the envelope.

Use the station stamp.

Example:

```md
[02-PR] 2026-05-12 — Flagged possible older pier-and-beam/foundation concern. Applied YELLOW slip and routed to Diana for inspector/professional verification before client advice.
```

---

## Rule 11: Buyer Intake Flags Are Required Operational Facts

Every buyer envelope must populate these fields:

- Preapproval Status
- Budget / Price Range
- Buyer-Stated Urgency
- First-Touch Acknowledgment Sent

If any Buyer Intake Flag is missing or Unknown, apply a YELLOW slip until the field is populated or explicitly marked Not Applicable.

---

## Rule 12: Same-Day Urgency

Same-day buyer requests require a fast acknowledgment draft, not a showing confirmation.

A fast acknowledgment draft may go to `03_client_communication` in parallel with intake at `01_lead_qualifier`. The draft must still pass human approval before sending.

A fast acknowledgment never replaces buyer-representation/compliance clearance or scheduling clearance.

---

## Rule 13: BLUE Slip Destination

When applying a BLUE slip, name the verification destination when possible.

Example:

```md
BLUE slip — historic/zoning verification needed. See AUSTIN_RESOURCES.md → City of Austin zoning and historic preservation resources.
```

Not:

```md
BLUE slip — check zoning somewhere.
```
